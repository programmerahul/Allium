SMART STORAGE

About Project

Post-Harvest Losses in storage is a major issue in world. In India alone post harvest losses of onion in storage is around 13.4% . To address this problem the study focuses on the minimization of post harvest storage losses in onion. An IOT based model is developed which provides real-time monitoring of the optimal conditions in storage/ warehouse of onions. The factors which determine the life of onions are mainly temperature, humidity and release of gases. Based on these three conditions we have applied fuzzy logic to predict the storage life span of onion. Experiment result shows that the proposed system shows increase in the shelf life of onions. We have implemented an Onion Storage monitoring and controlling system based on IoT and Fuzzy Logic. Based on the performance of the system after implementation, it can be concluded that this system can monitor the onion storage in real-time and effective to increase shelf life of onion. Also, website helps the farmer to assess the conditions more efficiently. Moreover, this made system could also predict the shelf life of onions which far out-perform the conventional system
